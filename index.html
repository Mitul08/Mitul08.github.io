IP-to-Bridge Allocation Optimizer 🏅 Trade Secret · ST 2026
OR-Tools CP-SATPythonPandasConstraint programmingSoC design
The problem
Mapping SoC IP blocks to bridge resources manually couldn't guarantee constraint satisfaction across bandwidth limits, slot capacity, protocol compatibility, and frequency matching simultaneously.
What I built
A two-phase constraint solver using Google OR-Tools CP-SAT. Phase A finds the minimum number of bridges needed. Phase B uses no-good constraints to enumerate every unique valid allocation, giving architects a full set of feasible configurations to evaluate.
The outcome
Replaced the manual mapping process entirely. Delivered with Excel-driven input, fail-fast validation, and a structured output workbook with bridge utilization and balance metrics. Filed as a trade secret at STMicroelectronics (2026).
